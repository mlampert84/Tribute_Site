"It is perhaps very rhapsodic to only view the sensuous world as the manner in which the non-sensuous world appears, to view it as only an expression, a cipher of the non-sensuous world that must be puzzled out; however, it is nevertheless a very interesting idea and, when one really imagines it, a beautiful hope to think that we can decode this language of nature more and more."

--Wilhelm von Humbolt in a letter to Georg Forster from 1789
